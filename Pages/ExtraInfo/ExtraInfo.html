Extra Info

Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System (Home galaxy). It is a barred spiral galaxy.

Stars

Stars are luminous spheres of plasma held together by gravity, generating energy through nuclear fusion.

Solar System

Our Solar System consists of the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and comets orbiting around it.

Orbit

An orbit is the curved path of an object around a star, planet, or moon, governed by gravity.
